Calendar is my another open source project where I am trying to list events related to topics that most of the time I am working on (electronics/embedded/FPGA/software).

You can view each calendar (for now, only one calendar: Webinar) on this page. Furthermore, iCalendar (.ics) files provided for calendars. Subscribe

Calendars can be added your any calendar software supporting iCal.

Please do not download iCal files (.ics) and import them in your calendar. If you do this, you will get the current snapshot of calendars and once you import them your calendar will not be synced with our calendars in the future. You should add or import the iCal URLs, not the files!
